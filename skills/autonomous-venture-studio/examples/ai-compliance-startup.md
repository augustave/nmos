# AI Compliance Startup — 0-to-1

## Scenario

A partner has identified a gap: enterprises adopting AI need compliance tooling
that understands model behavior, not just policy documents. The studio will
validate the opportunity, design the product, build the brand, and produce
investor-ready narrative in one coordinated loop.

## Input

- Venture brief: "AI compliance for regulated industries — automate model risk assessment"
- Business constraints: $200k budget, 8-week sprint, must demo at board meeting
- Technical constraints: must integrate with existing MLOps (MLflow, Weights & Biases)
- Market signals: 12 enterprise interviews confirming manual audit pain, 3 RFPs in pipeline

## Output

- Validated market requirement: model risk scoring for financial services, healthcare first
- System architecture: API-first SaaS, pluggable model connectors, audit trail
- Brand system: "Governed AI" positioning, trust-forward visual identity
- Executive narrative: 12-slide deck + 2-minute demo video for board presentation
- Governance framework: decision rights, escalation paths, compliance boundaries

## Review Notes

The product scope must stay narrow enough to demo in 8 weeks. Do not let the
brand promise outrun what the MVP can actually deliver. Compliance positioning
requires legal review before any external-facing claims.
